Triplet SRC A little TLC for all

The Systems Research Centre1 where the triplet of Types, Logic ε̷ Categories are put first and foremost.

We plan to design and implement a programming language based on the Univalent Foundations and demonstrate it’s practicality by building principled, dependable software systems. For now, we’re not at all sure what that looks like… we need you!

Along the way, we will see how computational trinitarianism is generalised in light of Homotopy Type Theory. In particular, how the the triplet:

Types + Logic + Categories

generalises to

Types + Topologic + Topos 2

Whether you’re a programmer, logician or mathematician, we have some TLC for you — Types, Logic and Categories. We also have a little tender loving care for you—we strongly believe that when you care about the “developer experience”, you care about types, and logic, and categories.

We welcome collaboration with researchers, autodidacts, polymaths — you! Please also considering joing the centre. We have an open door policy but are currently not funded.
